A Story of Wonder

The Little Prince, was born in April 2009 from an exercise at the drama club at Sound Mental Health. As an ultimate story about universal goodness, it's not at all surprising why DD people loved it and chose it. It is a story about them wondering out in our world.

Discover the Essential

It is not an accident that they decided to end their play with Fox’s wisdom; Anything essential is invisible to the eyes, Wise people see only with their heart. Being relentlessly profiled taught them their values. Maybe we sometimes don’t understand them, but they don’t understand us either. The truth is: we actually complement each other and our coexistence is meant to be, so while we are teaching them our world, let’s learn some of theirs too.

What Does Tamed Mean?

It’s something being too often neglected. It means to create ties. For me, you are only a little boy like a thousand other little boys. For you I’m just a fox, like all of the other foxes. I have no need of you and you have no need of me either. But if you tame me, we will need each other; I’ll be the only fox in the World for you and you’ll be the only boy in the World for me. Please tame me.

You are not like my Rose. You're nothing like my Rose.

No-one has tamed you and you haven’t tame anyone. You’re lovely, but you’re empty. One couldn’t die for you. Of course, in the ordinary World, My Rose looks just like you. But my Rose is more important than all of you together. She’s the one that I put screen on, She’s the one that I watered, she’s the one that I killed butterflies for, she’s the one that I listened when she complained. And even sometimes when she said nothing at all. Since she is my Rose. My Rose!
